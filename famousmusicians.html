Elvis Presley

Born on January 8,1935. Elvis Aaron Presley, or simply Elvis, was an American singer and actor. He was dubbed the "King of Rock & Roll" and was regarded as one of the most signifacnt cultural figures of the 20th century. His interpretation of songs and provocative performance style combined with his potent mix of influences across color lines during a transformative era in race relations led him to great success. Some of his popular songs of which were "Heartbreak Hotel" & "Can't Help Falling In Love".

Micheal Jackson

Born on August 29,1958. Michael Jackson was a multi-talented musical entertainer, an American singer,songwriter,dancer & a philanthropist. He was dubbed as the "King of Pop" and is also regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century. Over his long career, his contributions to music made him a global figure. He influenced many artists across many genres. He also popularised complicated dance moves such as the "Moonwalk" as well as the "Robot". He is also the most awarded individual music artist in history. Some of his popular songs of which were "Billie Jean" & "Beat It".

Mariah Carey

Born on March 27,1969. Mariah Carey is an American singer & songwriter. She is referred to as the "Songbird Supreme" & the "Queen of Christmas". She is known for her five-octave vocal range, melismatic singing style and her whistle register. She rose to fame in 1990 with her debut album and was the first artist to have five singles which she released hit number one on the Billboard Hot 100. Some of her popular songs of which are "All I Want For Christmas Is You" & "Emotions".

Madonna

Born on August 16,1958. Madonna is an American singer & songwriter. Given the title of "Queen of Pop", she is renowned for her continual invention & versatility in music. She pushed the boundaries of artistic expression in music. She is a prominent cultural figure in both the 20th and 21st century. She remains as one of the most "well-documented figures of the modern age". Some of her popular songs of which are "Material Girl" & "Like A Virgin".

Beethoven

Born on 17 December,1770. Ludwig van Beethoven was a German composer & pianist. He still remains as one of the most admired composers in the history of Western Music. His works ranks amongst the most performed classical music & span the transition from Classical to Romantic era in classical music. He began to grow increasingly deaf during the time of which he showed an individual development of the styles of Joseph Haydn & Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. Some of his popular works of art are "Symphony No.5" & "Symphony No.6".
